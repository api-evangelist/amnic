This comprehensive guide evaluates eleven leading cloud cost management platforms, explaining that effective cost management answers three critical questions: where money was spent, who owns it, and whether spending aligned with budgets. Tools range from free native options like AWS Cost Explorer to enterprise solutions like Apptio Cloudability, with Amnic positioned as the strongest choice for teams requiring unified visibility across AWS, Azure, GCP, Oracle, and Alibaba alongside Kubernetes environments. The article provides a clear comparison framework to help organizations select the right solution for their scale and requirements.
