This article compares six leading CloudHealth alternatives for FinOps teams following Broadcom's acquisition of VMware, which has led many organizations to explore alternatives due to CloudHealth's steep pricing of approximately 2.5% of tracked cloud spend. The guide ranks Amnic, IBM Cloudability, CloudZero, Vantage, nOps, and Finout based on multi-cloud coverage, AI cost features, and pricing models. Teams are directed to the platform that best matches their specific needs and cloud infrastructure complexity.
